**Roles and Responsibilities**



The Online Book Store is designed with three distinct user roles: **User (Customer)**, **Seller (Organizer)**, and **Administrator (Admin).** Each role has specific permissions and responsibilities to ensure secure and efficient management of the platform.







**1. User (Customer)**



The **User** is the primary customer of the BookEase platform. Users can browse the book catalog, purchase books, manage their profiles, and track their orders.



**Responsibilities**



**User Registration**



\* Create a new account by providing personal information such as name, email address, and password.

\* Register securely using encrypted password storage.

\* Receive access to customer-specific features after successful registration.



**User Authentication**



\* Log in securely using registered credentials.

\* Access protected resources based on authentication.

\* Maintain secure user sessions.



**Profile Management**



\* View personal profile information.

\* Update name, email address, and password.

\* Maintain accurate account information.



**Book Browsing**



\* Browse all available books.

\* View detailed book information including title, author, genre, description, price, ratings, and availability.

\* Search books by title or author.

\* Filter books by genre, popularity, ratings, or price.



**Shopping Cart**



\* Add books to the shopping cart.

\* Update book quantities.

\* Remove books from the cart.

\* Review cart before checkout.



**Order Placement**



\* Purchase books securely.

\* View order summary before confirming.

\* Receive order confirmation with a unique order ID.



**Order History**



\* View previously purchased books.

\* Track order status.

\* Rate and review purchased books.



**Logout**



\* Securely log out of the BookEase application.

\* Terminate the current session to prevent unauthorized access.







**2. Seller (Organizer)**



The **Seller** is responsible for managing book inventory and fulfilling customer orders. Sellers maintain accurate product information and ensure timely order processing.



**Responsibilities**



**Seller Registration**



\* Register as a seller by providing business information.

\* Submit business name, email address, and password.

\* Await administrator approval before accessing seller features.



**Seller Authentication**



\* Log in securely using seller credentials.

\* Access seller-specific dashboard.



**Profile Management**



\* Update business information.

\* Modify business name, email, and password.

\* Maintain seller account details.



**Book Listing**



\* Add new books to the platform.

\* Upload book information including:



&#x20; \* Title

&#x20; \* Author

&#x20; \* Genre

&#x20; \* Description

&#x20; \* Price

&#x20; \* Quantity

&#x20; \* Cover Image (optional)



**Inventory Management**



\* Update stock quantities.

\* Modify book prices.

\* Edit descriptions.

\* Remove unavailable books.

\* Maintain accurate inventory records.



**Order Fulfillment**



\* View incoming customer orders.

\* Verify stock availability.

\* Prepare books for shipment.

\* Update order status during processing.

\* Ensure timely delivery of purchased books.



**Sales Monitoring**



\* View listed books.

\* Monitor inventory levels.

\* Track completed orders.

\* Analyze sales performance.



**Logout**



\* Securely log out of the BookEase application.







**3. Administrator (Admin)**



The **Administrator** has complete control over the BookEase platform. The Admin manages users, sellers, books, system security, and overall platform operations.



**Responsibilities**



**System Management**



\* Monitor overall application performance.

\* Configure system settings.

\* Maintain platform security.

\* Ensure uninterrupted service availability.



**User Management**



\* View all registered users.

\* Create new user accounts if required.

\* Update user information.

\* Delete inactive or unauthorized users.

\* Manage user ratings and reviews.



**Seller Management**



\* Review new seller registration requests.

\* Approve or reject seller accounts.

\* Update seller information.

\* Suspend or remove sellers if necessary.

\* Monitor seller ratings and performance.



**Book Management**



\* View all books listed on the platform.

\* Add new books when required.

\* Update existing book information.

\* Remove inappropriate or unavailable books.

\* Monitor inventory across all sellers.



**Order Management**



\* Monitor customer orders.

\* Resolve order-related issues.

\* Track order status.

\* Ensure smooth order processing.



**Dashboard Monitoring**



\* View platform statistics.

\* Monitor active users.

\* Monitor active sellers.

\* Track total books.

\* Analyze order activity.

\* Generate reports for system management.



**Security Management**



\* Manage user roles and permissions.

\* Prevent unauthorized access.

\* Maintain authentication policies.

\* Monitor suspicious activities.



**Logout**



\* Securely log out of the BookEase application after completing administrative tasks.







**Role Summary**



| Role                      | Primary Responsibilities                                                                                                                                                 |

|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |

|     **User (Customer)**       | Register, log in, browse books, search books, manage profile, add books to cart, place orders, view order history, rate books, and log out securely.                     



|     **Seller (Organizer)**    | Register as a seller, manage profile, add and update book listings, maintain inventory, process customer orders, fulfill shipments, monitor sales, and log out 



|     **Administrator (Admin)** | Manage users, sellers, books, inventory, orders, security, platform settings, dashboard analytics, and overall system administration.
